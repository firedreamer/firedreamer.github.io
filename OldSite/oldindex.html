OpenGames
is the commitment to making the source code to the pre-content version of games. It allows beginners in gamedev to start from a pre-made foundation, and enthusiasts to observe how certain features are implemented.

Spatialsurf
Originally developed in 72 hours for Ludum Dare 41, securing #244 off of over 3000 entries from all over the world. It has been recently revamped for Android, and has been made open source under the Mozilla Public License as a part of the OpenGames initiative.

XRandR Scale Generator
Many people do not use full-HD monitors, and small resolution monitors are a pain to use. Users of the XOrg display server can simulate virtual super resolution using the XRandR utility. This PHP script generates an XRandR command to be used for super or subscaling your display.
See also: xrDS, a nifty keybind for switching between resolutions.

About

Who is TheAidency?
TheAidency is a 19(almost 20~!) year-old game developer, tech-enthusiast and FLOSS advocate. He has developed multiple short games from 2018, though he has dabbled in the world of game development since 2013. He has also worked on multiple AR apps for the now-liquidated AR startup Aabhas, been a focus tester for the e-sports app eBlitz, and is a feverent Ludum Dare jammer. He has released multiple games on itch.io and the Google Play store, and believes in de-mistifying technology and using games as a medium to express narratives, themes and concepts, with the intent of provoking some stream of thought in his players. At present, he is majoring in Computer Science(hardly a year left!), besides working on his not-exactly-first large-scale pipe dream side project, Damage Done.
