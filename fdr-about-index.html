For The ❤️ of Gaming

Firedreamer is a new indie developer creating interactive experiences using games and game technology as a medium to express narratives, themes and concepts, with the intent of provoking some stream of thought in our players. We're currently working on Damage Done: a third person tactical espionage action game where the player can possess NPCs.

What We're About

Passion

Games are a lot like an interaction between a player and the devs. We put our heart and soul into our projects, making them an expression of the self- and then some.

Freedom

We believe in the adage, "If you love something, set it free." Firedreamer has a very permissive IP policy, and most of our projects are open source, under the Mozilla Public License 2.0.

Quality

Dedicated to making the most of our resources, we strive to reach the highest possible level for our craft, using copious amounts of polish to make every little part of the projext shine.
